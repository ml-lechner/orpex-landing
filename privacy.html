Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2025

Orpex Technologies, Inc. ("Orpex," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform at orpex.ai and related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, company name, and role when you register.
  • Company Formation Data: Business entity details, founder information, equity allocations, and formation preferences you submit through our incorporation flow.
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through our third-party payment processor (Stripe). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Communications: Messages you send through our chat interface, support requests, and feedback.

1.2 Information from Integrations

When you connect third-party services, we access data as authorized through OAuth:

  • Email (Gmail): Email metadata, subjects, bodies, and attachments to provide company context to your AI agents.
  • Slack: Messages, channels, and communication data to enable operational context.
  • Calendar (Google Calendar): Event titles, times, attendees, and descriptions for scheduling coordination.
  • Financial Services: Transaction data from connected banking and bookkeeping services for financial monitoring.

You can revoke access to any integration at any time through your Settings page.

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps, and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for authentication and optional analytics cookies with your consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service, including AI agent operations
  • Process company formation requests through our partners
  • Enable AI agents to operate with your company context
  • Generate documents, reports, and operational insights
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and support
  • Improve our Service through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Protect Your Information

  • Encryption at Rest: All stored data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transfers use TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Token Security: OAuth tokens are encrypted with AES-128 (Fernet) before storage.
  • Data Isolation: Company data is logically isolated — no cross-tenant access.
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with principle of least privilege.
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on SOC 2 compliant infrastructure providers (Railway, Supabase, Vercel).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate (hosting, payment processing, company formation). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • AI Processing: Your company context is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for AI agent operations. Data is processed according to Anthropic's data processing terms and is not used to train models.
  • Formation Partners: Company formation data is shared with our incorporation partner (Doola) to process your filing.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to affected users.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Revoke Integrations: Disconnect any third-party service at any time.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of non-essential communications and analytics cookies.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@orpex.ai.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., tax records, formation documents). Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

7. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. We implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the Service. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
